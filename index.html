Introducing Hummingbird

With our on-demand travel service, passengers will see reduced travel times all the while reducing our carbon footprint by implementing smart routes that significantly minimize unnecessary mileage and maximize the efficiency of each journey.

Scheduled pick-ups

Plan your journeys ahead with our Scheduled Pickups. Simply select your preferred bus stop and pickup time, and we'll be there, right on schedule. Travel made predictable, just for you.

Quickest routes

Zoom past conventional routes with our Quickest Routes feature. Your ride goes straight to the destination, pausing only for pre-booked pickups or drop-offs. Dynamic, efficient and direct. That's travel done right.

Money and climate smart

By taking the quickest routes, we conserve energy, cut down fares, and contribute to a greener planet. Save your money and the environment, in one ride.

How to use your Hummingbird

Reserve

Jump on the app and book a ride. Choose your preferred pickup location, destination, and desired time. We'll handle the rest, scheduling your ride efficiently to meet your needs.

Ride

Step onto your reserved bus and relax. Our intelligent system ensures the quickest routes, dynamically adapting in real-time to get you straight to your destination.

Enjoy

Savor your journey knowing you're not just saving time and money, but also contributing to a greener planet. Enjoy the convenience, embrace the savings, and appreciate your part in environmental conservation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our app operates in real-time and on-demand. As soon as a user books a bus, our system crafts the most efficient route to their destination. This route takes into consideration the locations of other passengers and their respective stops. Instead of stopping at every single bus stop, the bus only makes necessary stops, picking up and dropping off passengers exactly where they need to be.

Our advanced AI technology estimates travel times by evaluating a host of factors including dynamic pick-ups and drop-offs, current weather conditions, and traffic flow. This multi-faceted approach allows us to provide accurate travel time estimates to our users.

You can easily reserve a bus ride either through the app or directly at a bus stop. Each bus stop is equipped with a button that, when pressed, alerts the bus driver of your pickup request.

Our buses run on electric power, which significantly reduces carbon emissions. Moreover, because our routes are tailored to passenger demand, we minimize unnecessary travel and energy usage, leading to cost savings for transportation companies.

Our app is designed to be inclusive and accessible. It's compatible with all major operating systems including iOS, Android, and Windows. We also ensure it works seamlessly across a variety of devices such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops.

We place utmost importance on user privacy and data security. Our app uses advanced encryption technology to protect user data. We strictly adhere to privacy regulations and guidelines, and we do not share user data without explicit consent. We also conduct regular audits and updates to enhance our security measures and ensure user data remains safe and secure.
